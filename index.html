We are a family of dog people. Our Black Labs, Daisy and Lola, come with us everywhere they are allowed, but when it comes to eating and drinking, the list of dog-friendly places in New York City is sadly very short. So, we decided to create a space where New Yorkers like us could enjoy the company of their pups over an expertly prepared cup of coffee or a casual glass of wine. Enter Black Lab Café: a safe, dog-friendly environment on the Upper West Side with tasty treats for humans and canines alike.
